# Dynamic Tagging and Categorization

1. Parse posts, comments, and attachments for topic signals.
2. Apply taxonomy rules and semantic classification labels.
3. Resolve duplicates, synonym conflicts, and hierarchy collisions.
4. Assign confidence scores and human-review flags.
5. Update archive indexes and search metadata.
6. Re-tag stale content as taxonomy evolves.
7. Output indexing health metrics and uncovered-content report.
