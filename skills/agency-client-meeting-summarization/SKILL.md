# Client Meeting Summarization

1. Ingest meeting transcript, notes, and agenda context.
2. Extract decisions, risks, blockers, and commitments.
3. Assign action items with owner and due date.
4. Format summary for client-facing and internal variants.
5. Push updates to project management tools.
6. Flag unresolved items needing follow-up.
7. Output meeting brief with confirmation checklist.
