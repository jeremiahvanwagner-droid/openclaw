# Dynamic Sponsorship Pricing

1. Gather current audience metrics, engagement quality, and campaign outcomes.
2. Benchmark pricing against comparable creator deals.
3. Model value contribution across deliverable formats.
4. Calculate dynamic price bands by package and channel.
5. Adjust quotes based on demand, seasonality, and exclusivity.
6. Generate negotiation-ready pricing rationale.
7. Output pricing matrix and deal-floor guidance.
