# Automated Accountability

1. Load client commitments, due dates, and preferred channel settings.
2. Schedule nudge cadence by task priority and urgency.
3. Send reminders with concise, action-oriented prompts.
4. Capture responses and completion confirmations.
5. Escalate overdue critical tasks with stronger prompts.
6. Adjust cadence based on client responsiveness.
7. Output accountability compliance report.
