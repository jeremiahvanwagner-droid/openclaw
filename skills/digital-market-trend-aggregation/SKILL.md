# Market Trend Aggregation

1. Define the niche universe and keyword seed set.
2. Collect signals from approved public sources (search trends, social velocity, marketplace listings, forums, reviews).
3. Normalize raw metrics into a common scoring frame (volume, growth rate, competition density, intent quality).
4. Calculate opportunity scores by niche and rank top candidates.
5. Flag compliance, IP, and brand-risk constraints before recommendations.
6. Output a prioritized list with evidence links, confidence level, and a 7-day test plan.
7. Recommend one low-risk validation experiment per top niche.
