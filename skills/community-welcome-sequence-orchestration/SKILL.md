# Welcome Sequence Orchestration

1. Trigger onboarding flow at membership activation.
2. Deliver stepwise orientation content and key navigation guides.
3. Introduce community norms, values, and participation expectations.
4. Prompt first-win actions to accelerate activation.
5. Route members to relevant channels, groups, or cohorts.
6. Detect onboarding stalls and send recovery nudges.
7. Output activation metrics and onboarding bottleneck insights.
