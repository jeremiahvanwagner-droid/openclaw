# Cart Abandonment Recovery

1. Detect abandonment events and classify by value and intent signals.
2. Segment users by prior purchases, price sensitivity, and engagement history.
3. Trigger recovery sequence timing by abandonment context.
4. Personalize reminder content with product context and urgency-safe messaging.
5. Apply incentive rules only where margin thresholds allow.
6. Track recovery outcomes across touchpoints.
7. Output recovered revenue report and sequence optimization actions.
