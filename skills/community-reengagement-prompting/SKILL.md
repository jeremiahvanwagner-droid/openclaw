# Re-engagement Prompting

1. Identify dormant members by inactivity thresholds.
2. Profile prior interests, milestones, and engagement history.
3. Generate personalized prompts tied to member intent.
4. Select optimal channel and send-time window.
5. Sequence follow-ups with escalating relevance, not pressure.
6. Capture response outcomes and update engagement state.
7. Output reactivation performance and next-action queue.
