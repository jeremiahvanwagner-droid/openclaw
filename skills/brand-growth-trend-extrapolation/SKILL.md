# Growth Trend Extrapolation

1. Compile historical audience and revenue trend data.
2. Model growth scenarios under conservative/base/aggressive assumptions.
3. Estimate monetization capacity by channel and offer type.
4. Project sponsorship value ranges based on reach and engagement.
5. Highlight confidence bounds and risk factors.
6. Recommend target ranges for next planning cycle.
7. Output forecast report with negotiation guidance.
