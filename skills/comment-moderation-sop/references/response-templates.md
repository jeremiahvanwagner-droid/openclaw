# Response Templates

## Question
- Short: "Great question — here’s the quick answer: [answer]."
- Empathetic: "Thanks for asking. I can see why that matters — here’s what we can share: [answer]."
- Authority: "Based on our current policy/process, the correct next step is: [answer]."

## Objection
- Short: "Fair point. The key difference is [specific mechanism/proof]."
- Empathetic: "Totally valid concern. Most people ask this before they see [proof/clarification]."
- Authority: "We avoid hype; here are the verifiable facts: [fact 1], [fact 2]."

## Sensitive (Escalate)
- Short: "Thanks for flagging this — we’re escalating to support for accurate follow-up."
- Empathetic: "I hear you. We’re moving this to the right team so you get a proper response."
- Authority: "For accuracy and privacy, this needs formal review. Please use [support channel]."

## Hostile (De-escalation)
- Short: "I’m here to help if you want to discuss specifics respectfully."
- Empathetic: "I understand you’re frustrated. Share details and we’ll review constructively."
- Authority: "We’ll continue if we can keep this respectful and focused on facts."

## Refund/Payment
- Short: "Please contact [billing link] so we can verify and resolve this quickly."
- Empathetic: "Sorry for the friction — billing can help immediately at [billing link]."
- Authority: "For account security, payment issues are handled only through [billing link]."
