# Pipeline Velocity Tracking

1. Ingest opportunities, stage transitions, and timestamp history.
2. Compute stage duration benchmarks by segment.
3. Detect stalled deals and slow-moving bottleneck stages.
4. Attribute delays to known friction causes.
5. Prioritize interventions for high-value delayed opportunities.
6. Track changes after intervention actions.
7. Output velocity dashboard with conversion acceleration recommendations.
