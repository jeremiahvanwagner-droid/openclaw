# Knowledge Decay Prediction

1. Collect mastery timestamps, recall checks, and revision history.
2. Estimate retention half-life by topic and learner profile.
3. Forecast decay risk windows for each competency.
4. Schedule refresher micro-lessons before predicted retention cliffs.
5. Prioritize refreshers for high-impact competencies first.
6. Adapt future intervals based on refresher performance.
7. Output retention dashboard with upcoming intervention schedule.
