# Brand Governance Enforcement

1. Load brand values, red lines, and approved claim boundaries.
2. Scan content for policy, tone, and positioning violations.
3. Flag high-risk statements requiring revision.
4. Apply corrective rewrites to restore alignment.
5. Escalate unresolved high-risk outputs for human approval.
6. Log governance decisions for audit trail.
7. Output approved content with governance status.
