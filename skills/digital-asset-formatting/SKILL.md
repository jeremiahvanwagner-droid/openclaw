# Digital Asset Formatting

1. Ingest raw source material and classify by module/topic.
2. Select output formats required by the product package.
3. Transform content into standardized templates with consistent structure.
4. Apply formatting rules for readability, hierarchy, and brand voice.
5. Validate links, references, and asset completeness.
6. Produce final packaged deliverables with version labeling.
7. Output release bundle checklist and missing-item report.
