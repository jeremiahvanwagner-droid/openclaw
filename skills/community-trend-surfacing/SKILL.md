# Community Trend Surfacing

1. Aggregate recurring topics across posts, comments, and support threads.
2. Cluster related requests into trend themes.
3. Score trends by frequency, growth rate, and strategic impact.
4. Distinguish short-lived noise from persistent demand.
5. Map trends to existing offers, gaps, or roadmap candidates.
6. Generate evidence-backed recommendations for owners.
7. Output weekly trend brief with priority ranking.
