# Cohort Pacing Synchronization

1. Define cohort milestones, deadlines, and group activity windows.
2. Monitor member progress variance against cohort timeline.
3. Detect pacing drift that threatens collaboration readiness.
4. Recommend catch-up or stretch actions by learner segment.
5. Adjust cohort reminders and release gates to preserve alignment.
6. Coordinate live session prep with current competency readiness.
7. Output pacing health report and intervention queue.
