# Resource Utilization Tracking

1. Aggregate workload assignments, throughput, and capacity limits.
2. Compute utilization rates by resource and team function.
3. Detect over-allocation and under-utilization patterns.
4. Recommend rebalancing actions to protect SLAs.
5. Simulate allocation changes before applying.
6. Monitor post-reallocation performance.
7. Output utilization heatmap and staffing recommendations.
