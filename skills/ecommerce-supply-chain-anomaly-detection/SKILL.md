# Supply Chain Anomaly Detection

1. Monitor shipment events, carrier feeds, supplier ETAs, and warehouse status.
2. Detect anomalies against expected transit and fulfillment baselines.
3. Classify disruption type and severity.
4. Trigger predefined mitigations (reroute, split shipment, supplier fallback).
5. Notify stakeholders based on impact thresholds.
6. Track incident resolution and SLA impact.
7. Output disruption report with preventive recommendations.
