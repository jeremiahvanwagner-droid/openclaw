# Volatility Stress Testing

1. Define baseline portfolio state and exposure vectors.
2. Generate shock scenarios across price, spread, and liquidity dimensions.
3. Simulate P&L, drawdown, and margin impacts.
4. Evaluate breach risk for limits and covenants.
5. Compare results to approved risk appetite thresholds.
6. Recommend size adjustments or hedges before execution.
7. Output stress report with go/no-go status.
