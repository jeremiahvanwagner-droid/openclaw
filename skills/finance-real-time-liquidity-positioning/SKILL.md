# Real-time Liquidity Positioning

1. Aggregate current balances, obligations, and settlement windows.
2. Classify liquidity tiers by immediacy and risk tolerance.
3. Forecast near-term cash demands and collateral requirements.
4. Reallocate idle capital into approved liquidity venues.
5. Enforce concentration and counterparty limits.
6. Recompute allocation after material market or flow changes.
7. Output liquidity posture report with utilization metrics.
