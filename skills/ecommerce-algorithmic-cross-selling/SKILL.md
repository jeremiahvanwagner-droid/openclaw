# Algorithmic Cross-selling

1. Build product affinity graph from order and browsing behavior.
2. Detect current shopper intent from cart and session context.
3. Select candidate add-ons with compatibility and margin filters.
4. Re-rank offers by relevance, profitability, and conversion likelihood.
5. Suppress repetitive or low-quality recommendations.
6. Deliver placement-specific variants (PDP, cart, checkout, post-purchase).
7. Output uplift metrics and recommendation quality report.
