# Student Churn Prediction

1. Aggregate engagement, progress, attendance, and support interaction signals.
2. Compute churn risk score with explainable feature contributions.
3. Classify risk tiers and identify likely dropout triggers.
4. Generate intervention actions per risk profile.
5. Trigger outreach workflows with urgency based on risk window.
6. Track intervention outcomes and recalibrate model thresholds.
7. Output retention queue with prioritized case list.
