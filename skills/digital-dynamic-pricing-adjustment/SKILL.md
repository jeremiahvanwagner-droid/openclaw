# Dynamic Pricing Adjustment

1. Gather baseline price, conversion, refund rate, and revenue per visitor.
2. Detect demand regime changes using traffic velocity and purchase intent signals.
3. Model approved price bands and guardrails (floor, ceiling, brand constraints).
4. Simulate impact across AOV, unit sales, and net revenue.
5. Select price action (hold, step-up, step-down, tier rebalance) with rationale.
6. Schedule time-bound pricing windows and rollback triggers.
7. Output recommended change, risk note, and monitoring checklist.
