# Contract Risk Extraction

1. Parse agreement text into clause-level sections.
2. Identify high-risk terms (indemnity, IP transfer, unlimited liability, penalties).
3. Compare clauses against approved fallback language.
4. Score risk severity and negotiation priority.
5. Draft redline recommendations for non-standard clauses.
6. Flag clauses requiring legal escalation.
7. Output risk memo with clause references and action plan.
