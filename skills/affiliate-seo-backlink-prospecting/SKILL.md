# Backlink Prospecting

1. Define target pages and topical authority goals.
2. Identify relevant domains with link equity and editorial fit.
3. Score prospects by authority, relevance, and outreach likelihood.
4. Exclude spammy or risky targets.
5. Generate outreach-ready prospect lists with contact context.
6. Segment by partnership type and intent.
7. Output prioritized outreach pipeline with rationale.
