# Voice Consistency Enforcement

1. Load approved voice profile, style rules, and prohibited phrasing.
2. Analyze draft output for tone, rhythm, and diction alignment.
3. Detect deviations from signature voice patterns.
4. Rewrite content to restore brand-authentic expression.
5. Validate emotional tone against content intent.
6. Score voice fidelity before release.
7. Output final copy with consistency confidence.
