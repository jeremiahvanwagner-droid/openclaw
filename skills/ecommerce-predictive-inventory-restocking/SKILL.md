# Predictive Inventory Restocking

1. Collect SKU sales velocity, seasonality, lead times, and safety stock rules.
2. Forecast demand windows using recent trend and historical baseline.
3. Estimate stockout risk date per SKU.
4. Compute reorder points and optimal order quantities.
5. Generate draft purchase orders for high-risk SKUs.
6. Apply supplier constraints and minimum order rules.
7. Output prioritized replenishment queue with ETA impact.
