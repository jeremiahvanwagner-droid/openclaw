# Customer Avatar Synthesis

1. Compile customer evidence from conversations, reviews, support logs, and conversion data.
2. Segment by goals, constraints, objections, and readiness level.
3. Build avatar profiles with pains, triggers, language patterns, and decision criteria.
4. Extract exact voice-of-customer phrases for messaging reuse.
5. Map avatars to relevant offers and funnel entry points.
6. Produce messaging guidelines by segment (hooks, promises, objections).
7. Output avatar pack with do-not-use language and compliance notes.
