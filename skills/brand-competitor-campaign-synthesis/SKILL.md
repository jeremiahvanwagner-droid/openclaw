# Competitor Campaign Synthesis

1. Monitor competitor launches, partnerships, and promotional cycles.
2. Extract campaign mechanics and monetization structures.
3. Compare against current brand positioning and audience fit.
4. Identify transferable tactics and white-space opportunities.
5. Rank experiments by impact, effort, and risk.
6. Draft test briefs with success criteria.
7. Output monetization opportunity backlog.
