# Conversational Role-play

1. Select scenario goals, context, and target soft skills.
2. Instantiate role personas with realistic constraints and behaviors.
3. Conduct turn-based dialogue with adaptive difficulty.
4. Score learner responses on clarity, empathy, structure, and outcome quality.
5. Provide immediate coaching prompts during low-performance turns.
6. Deliver post-session debrief with strengths and improvement actions.
7. Recommend next scenario based on observed weaknesses.
