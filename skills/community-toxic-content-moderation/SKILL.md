# Toxic Content Moderation

1. Scan incoming content against policy and abuse detection rules.
2. Score severity for spam, harassment, hate, fraud, and prohibited content.
3. Apply policy action by tier (allow, warn, hide, remove, escalate).
4. Preserve evidence logs for audit and appeal workflows.
5. Trigger moderator escalation for ambiguous or high-risk cases.
6. Rate-limit repeat offenders and apply progressive enforcement.
7. Output moderation summary with false-positive review queue.
