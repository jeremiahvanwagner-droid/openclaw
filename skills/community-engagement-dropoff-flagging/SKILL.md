# Engagement Drop-off Flagging

1. Baseline normal engagement cadence by member segment.
2. Track declines in activity, session frequency, and contribution depth.
3. Compute drop-off risk score with explainable drivers.
4. Classify risk tiers and intervention urgency windows.
5. Trigger retention workflows for high-risk members.
6. Monitor intervention outcomes and adjust thresholds.
7. Output prioritized re-engagement list for operators.
