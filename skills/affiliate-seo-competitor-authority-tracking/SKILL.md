# Competitor Authority Tracking

1. Define competitor set and monitored authority signals.
2. Track domain/page authority, link growth, and ranking footprint.
3. Detect notable strategy shifts (new clusters, link bursts, format changes).
4. Compare visibility movement versus owned properties.
5. Identify exploitable weaknesses and white-space opportunities.
6. Recommend content and link-building responses.
7. Output competitive landscape brief with action priorities.
