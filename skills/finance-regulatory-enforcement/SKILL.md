# Regulatory Enforcement

1. Screen counterparties and wallets against approved watchlists.
2. Validate jurisdiction, product, and policy eligibility rules.
3. Detect restricted transaction patterns and sanctioned exposure.
4. Block or quarantine non-compliant actions automatically.
5. Trigger compliance escalation for ambiguous cases.
6. Log all decisions with evidentiary metadata.
7. Output compliance status and exception queue.
