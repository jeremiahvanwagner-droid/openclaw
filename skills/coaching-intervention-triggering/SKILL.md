# Intervention Triggering

1. Monitor signals for distress, freeze, or repeated breakdown patterns.
2. Score intervention urgency using predefined thresholds.
3. Distinguish coachable resistance from high-risk emotional states.
4. Trigger escalation workflow to designated human expert.
5. Preserve relevant context for handoff clarity.
6. Pause unsafe autonomous prompts until reviewed.
7. Output intervention log and resolution status.
