# Social Listening Alerting

1. Track brand mentions, keywords, and related entities across channels.
2. Detect unusual mention velocity and sentiment spikes.
3. Classify events as risk, opportunity, or neutral noise.
4. Trigger alerts based on urgency and impact thresholds.
5. Recommend response playbook actions per event type.
6. Monitor event evolution and alert status changes.
7. Output incident timeline and resolution summary.
